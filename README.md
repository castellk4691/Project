# Prototype MVP

## What It Does
It gives singers cover ideas cover for TikTok posts by the user providing the song name, artist, and the asthetic/vibe they're going for. The bot gives 2 color palette options, video editing directions, typography suggestion, caption, and a short list of hashtags.

## How to Use
1. Type the song and artist name
2. Type the want you want
3. Click "Generate My Vibe"
4. Copy and paste captions and hastags
5. Use suggested font style, color palette, and video editing direction to edit video and cover for posts

## Features Implemented
- Vibe Input: Learns your song and mood.
- Dual Mood Boards: Generates two unique color palettes and visual directions.
- Tailored Captions: Writes unique text based on your specific song and vibe.
- Smart Hashtags: Auto-creates tags for the artist, song, and sub-genre.
- Typography: Suggests specific font styles to match the look.
- Modern Design: Mobile-friendly, sleek, and fast.
- Privacy-First: Works entirely offline in your browser.
